## What Are Challenges?

Challenges are longer-term, structured goals you want to track separately from daily quests. Unlike quests that reset daily/weekly, challenges persist until you complete them!

### Completing Items

**For Checklists (75 Hard, Daily Checklist):**
- Check off items as you complete them
- When all items are checked, the day advances
- Checklist resets for the next day

**For Progress Trackers (Movies, Crafts, Custom Lists):**
- Check off items as you complete them
- Items stay checked (they don't reset)
- Add new items with the "+ Add" button

### Adding Items

For list-based challenges (Movies, Crafts):
1. Type in the input box at the bottom
2. Click "+ Add"
3. Item appears in the list

**Movie Format Examples:**

## Tips for Success

**Start Small:**
- Don't start 5 challenges at once
- Pick 1-2 that matter most
- Build momentum before adding more

**Be Realistic:**
- Set achievable goals
- It's okay to delete and restart
- Progress over perfection

**Make It Fun:**
- Theme your challenges (date nights, seasonal crafts)
- Share progress with friends/partner
- Celebrate milestones

**Track Your Why:**
- Add notes in the description
- Take photos along the way
- Reflect on what you learned

## Challenge vs. Quest vs. Collection Log

**Challenges:**
- Long-term structured goals (weeks/months)
- Checklist or progress tracking
- Examples: 75 Hard, watch 100 movies

**Quests:**
- Daily/weekly repeatable tasks
- Earn XP and level up skills
- Examples: workout, dev work, cooking

**Collection Log:**
- Unique experiences with photos
- One-time special moments
- Examples: date at fancy restaurant, first 5K

**When to Use What:**
- **Challenge**: "Watch all Christopher Nolan movies"
- **Quest**: "Have a movie night" (repeatable weekly)
- **Collection Log**: "First date at The French Laundry" (unique memory)
